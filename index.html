About

Maithili was born on 25 July 2000 in a small town called Benipatti, located in the Madhubani district ofBihar . Her father was Ramesh Thakur, a popular musician in his region , and mother Bharti Thakur, a housewife. She was named after her mother. She has two younger brothers, named Rishav and Ayachi, who follow their elder sister's musical journey by playing the tabla and accompanying her in singing. She learned music from her father. Realizing his daughter's potential and to get more opportunities, Ramesh Thakur based himself and his family in Dwarkanier New Delhi. Maithili and her two brothers were educated at the Bal Bhavan International School there. Even during their studies, the three siblings were trained by their father in Hindustani classical music, harmonium, and tabla (in Rishav's case). [3] Maithili's musical journey began in 2011, when she appeared in a reality show called Little Champs, aired in Zee TV. Although she had appeared in many local shows before, she gained recognition through this reality show. Four years later, she appeared in another reality show, Indian Idol Junior, aired in Sony TV. But she became a national sensation through the reality show Rising Star, in which she finished as the runner-up. Right from the beginning of the show, Maithili had immense popularity, having sung even challenging songs with ease. Her fame rose in 2017 when she participated in season 1 of Rising Star. Maithili was the first finalist of the show, singing 'Om Namah Shivaya' which gave her a direct entry into the finals. She finished second, losing by two votes. After the show, her internet popularity soared. Her videos onYouTubeandFacebookNow get between 70,000 and 7 million views. She is seen with her two younger brothers Rishabh and Ayachi. Rishabh is on the tabla and Ayachi is a singer. In 2019 Maithili and her two brothers were made the brand ambassadors of Madhubani by the Election Commission. [4] She won an Indian musicshow "I Genius Young Singing Star" in 2015 and also launched an album Ya Rabba (Universal Music, Her Facebook channel has more than 9 million followers and YouTube has more than 2.5 million subscribers and she has more than 2 million followers on Instagram.
